### DOCUMENT: COMMUNITY ROOM USAGE POLICY & AGREEMENT
**Venue:** Quartiersladen, Den-Haag-Straße 3
**Administered by:** Quartiersladen Team (Volunteer Neighbors)

---

### 1. BOOKING REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY INFO)
To confirm a booking, the user MUST provide the following details. If any are missing, the assistant must ask for them:
* **Full Name** (First and Last)
* **Organization** (Only if applicable)
* **Postal Address**
* **Email Address**
* *(Note: A phone number is NOT required).*

### 2. USAGE DEFINITIONS
**Permitted Usage Types:**
* One-time event
* Weekly recurring usage
* Monthly recurring usage

**Required Usage Details:**
* Specific Date and Time of usage
* Purpose of usage (Occasion)
* Estimated number of persons

### 3. COSTS & PAYMENT POLICY
**3.1 Usage Fees (Donation Basis)**
* **Policy:** There is NO fixed rental fee for using the room.
* **Donation:** The room operates on a voluntary donation basis. Users are encouraged to make a voluntary contribution ("Kostenbeitrag") to support the space.
* **Payment Method:** Donations are collected in **cash** upon key handover.

**3.2 Mandatory Costs**
* **Security Deposit (Kaution):** A mandatory **100 € cash** deposit is required at key handover. This is refundable if the room is returned clean and undamaged.
* **Cleaning Fee (Optional):** If the user does not wish to clean the room themselves, they may request a cleaning service for a flat fee of **20 €**.

### 4. USER OBLIGATIONS & RULES
By booking the room, the user agrees to the following strict rules:
* **Care of Property:** Users must treat the room and objects with utmost care.
* **Supervision:** Users are responsible for supervising any children or adolescents (under 18).
* **Damages:** Any damage to the room or equipment must be reported immediately. The user is liable for full repair or replacement costs.
* **Condition:** The room must be returned in a tidy, ventilated, and clean condition (unless the 20€ cleaning option was booked).
* **Liability:** If rules are violated, the 100€ deposit may be retained.

### 5. OPERATIONAL PROCEDURES (THE CHECKLIST)

#### 5.1 Before the Event (Key Handover)
* **Appointment:** A handover time must be agreed upon in advance.
* **Payment:** The 100€ Deposit and any voluntary donation must be paid in **cash**.
* **Instruction:** The user will receive instructions on using devices (dishwasher, projector, etc.).
* **Early Access:** The room MAY NOT be entered before the booked time (strictly no early setup or dropping off items).

#### 5.2 During the Event (Rules of Conduct)
* **Heating:** Do NOT adjust the thermostats.
* **Noise Control:** This is a residential building. Volume must be kept to "Room Volume" (Zimmerlautstärke). Consideration for neighbors is mandatory.
* **Smoking Policy:** Strict **NO SMOKING** inside the building. Smoking is allowed outside, but NOT directly in front of the entrance. Cigarette butts must be disposed of properly.
* **Curfew:** All events must end by **10:00 PM (22:00)** at the latest.
* **Support:** There is no on-site support during the event (the team are volunteers).

#### 5.3 After the Event (Cleanup & Departure)
* **Decorations:** Remove all decorations, tape, and brought materials completely.
* **Dishes:** Wash all dishes and cutlery. Put them away exactly as found.
* **Surfaces:** Clean work surfaces, sink, stove, and oven.
* **Electronics:** Turn off or unplug the projector, stove, dishwasher, and coffee machine.
* **Floors:** Sweep the floor. If necessary, wipe it damp (use ONLY the provided neutral cleaner).
* **Food:** Remove ALL food and drinks. The refrigerator must be empty and clean. Leave the fridge/beverage cabinet doors **OPEN**.
* **Bathroom:** Clean the toilet and sink. Put used towels in the laundry basket in the toilet.
* **Closing Up:** Ventilate the room well, turn off lights, and lock all windows and doors securely.

#### 5.4 Waste Disposal Rules (Strict)
* **ALLOWED in Building Trash:** Only paper waste, compost, and residual waste in normal household quantities.
* **MUST BE TAKEN HOME:**
    * Glass / Bottles (Empty or full)
    * Plastic packaging & Styrofoam
    * Bulky waste
    * Large amounts of garbage
    * Decoration materials
* *(Note: Directions to the local recycling island are in the room folder).*

### 6. RETURN PROTOCOL
* **Inspection:** The room and key will be inspected upon return.
* **Refund:** If the room is in agreed condition, the 100€ deposit is fully refunded.
* **Retention:** If damages are found or cleaning is required, a portion or all of the deposit will be retained.
